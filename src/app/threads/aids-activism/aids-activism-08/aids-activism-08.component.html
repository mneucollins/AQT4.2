Stuart Colby Freedman

“Stuart was my friend. He was my husband’s brother and my children’s uncle. He went around the world many times but never found himself. He never said ‘Good Night’ to someone he loved. He never heard ‘Good Morning’ from someone who loved him.

Stuart traveled alone, teaching. Preaching to his Fire Island Congregation, reading, guiding people around New York and around the work, helping them to see the beauty he saw.“

My brother-in-law tried very hard to do the right thing and I liked this quality most of all. I loved this man very much. I’m happy for the time I had with him and I’m glad he was part of my life and my family.“
