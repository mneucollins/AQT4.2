Richard Cahalane

“My name is Ben Lieberson. I’m six years old. My Dad’s real name was Rich Cahalane but I used to call him Iggy. He died when I was 3 years old. I made this panel for The AIDS Quilt because my Dad died of AIDS.“

—Ben Lieberson, Jan. 18, 1995

“Rich Cahalane was an extraordinary man. He was a social activist, a loving father, my best friend and husband.

Rich turned his rage at social and political injustice into powerful creative energy. Rich's gentle spirit lives on -- in those who loved him and the many people whose lives he touched who never knew him.”

—Debbie Lieberson (Ben's Mom)
