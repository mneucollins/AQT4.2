“By the time we completed the project – 2 a.m. on the day the Quilt exhibit opened – it was obvious that this quilt panel had deeply touched all those who worked on it. We came to the sobering realization that we’re not invincible --- we’re all cut from the same fabric and we’re all vulnerable to this disease.

  The creation of this panel was a community project. It was conceived by the Mono County AIDS Coordinator, designed and painted by Mammoth High School art students and assembled by a member of the Mammoth Hospital Board.”

Betsy Mellinger, Mammoth Hospital
