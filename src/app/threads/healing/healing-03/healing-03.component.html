Carlos Charles Karloz Morales

“When my 14 year-old daughter and I showed up at Santa Sophia…ready to start our AIDS Quilt for my friend Carlos…we were greeted by Sister Margaret and the most wonderful group of women. From that first day we bonded and developed a friendship that has continued to today.

As we put our quilts together…Some of us ironed, glued, cut, sewed, gave advice and free hugs. In our little world, we released the shame and pain we had been carrying around for sometimes.”

--Sara A. Souffrain
