LaVonne Lacey, Tavoris Lacey

“LaVonne was abandoned by her family and Annie Collins, her nurse, Godmother and friend, took care of her, loving her and carrying her church. Annie stood by her to the bitter end. Tavorius was a beautiful two year-old that loved to go to church and is best remembered clapping his little hands and singing ‘Halle’lujah.’ He was a healthy happy little boy who got sick and died so quickly. Once he was gone, his mother was ready to join him.””

--Alice Macomber
