Jeremy Augustin

“Jeremy Augustin was dearly loved by all of us at Connor’s Nursery, a residential home for infants and children with AIDS. Jeremy spent all four years of his entire life with us. Though he could not walk or talk, he could communicate very well with hand motions and he had a very special talent for making each of us feel as if we were his favorite. Jeremy had the most beautiful eyes and sweet smile ever given to a child.”
