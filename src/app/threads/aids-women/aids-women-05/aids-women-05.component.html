SHIRLEY ANN GAULZETTI

“It is our family’s hope that people may stop to wonder how a 64 year old woman died of AIDS. The answer is: with dignity, grace and love…just like she lived her life. I never thought I’d see my mom on television, talking about condoms. I’m sure she never expected to either. A local TV station had asked to interview her about the rising incidence of the virus among heterosexual women. She called me to ask if I was ready for her to talk about AIDS so publicly. Was I ready? She was ready. That’s all I needed to know.”

--Janita Gaulzetti
