PEGGY BUCKLIN

“My best friend, Nicole, and I met Peggy Bucklin in 1987. She was the leader of our Presbyterian Youth Group. Every life that Peggy touched was changed in some way. She taught us all what it meant to be strong and to believe in ourselves. She was bright and beautiful and we miss her every day. Peggy would want us to go on and not to cry but with this Quilt piece we offer our tears and our joy and our friend’s cherished memory for everyone to see and share with us. We love you, Peggy.”

--Pagan Morris & Nicole Evans-Blanck
