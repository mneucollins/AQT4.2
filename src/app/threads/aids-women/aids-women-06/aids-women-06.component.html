KRISTA BLAKE

“One of the most heart-wrenching experiences a grandfather must endure is the death of a beautiful, intelligent 22 year-old granddaughter that he deeply loves. It’s been said that grown men don’t cry, but you can believe me when I say that I cried and I still cry inside when I think about what has been lost. My granddaughter and I had a marvelous relationship. The love that engendered that relationship still endures and the dignity and strength with which she bore her affliction inspires and sustains me as I approach the winter of my life.”

--Krista’s Opa (Grandfather)
