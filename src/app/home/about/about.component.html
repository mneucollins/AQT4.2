About

AIDS Quilt Touch is the online digital expression of the 1.4 million square foot AIDS Memorial Quilt;

When fully launched in its 2.0 version later this year, AIDS Quilt Touch will allow visitors to not only search the Quilt, and learn about its history, but also to add comments, upload videos, photographs and audio, contributing their experiences and rembrances.

It is our goal that AIDS Quilt Touch, like the Quilt itself, will help honor and remember the lives lost while promoting HIV-AIDS awareness. We also hope it will be a model for public digital humanities research, design and production.
